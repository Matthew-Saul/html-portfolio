The Best Films According to Matthew

Three Random Good Films

A Serious Man

This is my favorite Cohen Brothers film. The content of this film seems the most personal to the Choen brothers of any of their films I've seen thus far. As a result, the film feels like it has the most emotional depth to me. And is just really well done all around.

Song Of The Sea

A very unique style of animation and my favorite of the films by Cartoon Saloon. It's steeped in folklore, as all of their films have been so far, and it's a deeply beautiful and tender story.

Given

A beautiful documentary about a family who takes a trip around the world, surfing, fishing, making things with their hands, and telling stories steeped in meaning. The father in the family took a similar trip with his father when he was very young. And he wanted his three year old son to have a similar experience. The film is narrated by the three year old.
